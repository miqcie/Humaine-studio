## Usage Notes

### Workflow
1. **Select a topic** from this list based on priority and current interest
2. **Update status** to "In Progress" when you start writing
3. **Create research notes** in `writing-topics/notes/[TASK-ID]-notes.md`
4. **Create draft** in `_drafts/[title].md`
5. **Publish** by moving to `_posts/YYYY-MM-DD-[title].md`
6. **Update status** to "Published" when live

### Research Notes Structure
For each topic, create a notes file: `writing-topics/notes/WFA-001-notes.md`

Example structure:
```markdown
# WFA-001: MCP Research Notes

## Key Points
- [Research findings]
- [Quotes to include]
- [Examples to reference]

## Sources
- [Links]
- [Papers]
- [Projects]

## Outline Ideas
- [Potential structure]
```
