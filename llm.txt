## Site Overview

Humaine Studio is the personal website and learning portfolio of Chris McConnell, documenting a journey of learning to code with curiosity, an English/MBA background, and no formal CS education. The site serves multiple purposes:

1. **Research Portfolio** - Blog posts exploring AI, automation, and technical topics
2. **Career Showcase** - Professional applications and work samples (PostHog cover letter, resume)
3. **Educational Tools** - Practical applications like the T1D insulin calculator
4. **Learning Journey** - Transparent documentation of learning process, including mistakes and growth

**Core Philosophy**: Curiosity beats formal CS education. Building in public to inspire other career changers.
